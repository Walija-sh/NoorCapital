License & Regulations

Noor Capital PSC is authorised and regulated by the Securities and Commodities Authority (SCA) in the United Arab Emirates, we have an active and functioning license to practice the following activities:

  • First Category: Dealing in Securities (License No: 305028)

    • Trading broker in the international markets
    • Trading broker of OTC derivatives and currencies in the spot market

  • Second Category: Dealing in Investment (License No: 608023)

    • Investment fund management
    • Portfolios management

  • Fifth Category: Arrangement and Advice (License No: 20200000119)

    • Financial advisor (issuance manager)
    • Financial consultations
